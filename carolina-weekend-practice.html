Your Weekend Challenge

Welcome, Carolina! This weekend you'll practice what you learned about Runway using There is / There are, making questions, and mastering the TH sound.

Each activity has a model sentence for you to follow. After reading it, you write your own version. Then you record yourself speaking — no notes, just you and your voice.

Send your final audios to your teacher on Monday. You've got this! ✨

Activity 2 — Describing Runway's Office
🎯 Goal: Describe things you see in Runway using "There is" and "There are". Read the model, then write 2 similar sentences about what's in Andy's office.
Model Sentences:
✓ There is a big desk in the reception.
✓ There are many magazines on the tables.
✓ There is a coffee machine near the elevator.
Write Your Own Sentences
✅ Feedback
Remember the pattern:
Singular (1 thing): There IS a desk.
Plural (2+ things): There ARE many magazines.

Saturday

15 min
Activity 3 — Ask Questions About Runway
🎯 Goal: Create questions using the patterns from class. Read the models, then write your own questions about The Devil Wears Prada or Runway.
Model Questions:
✓ Is there a coffee machine at Runway?
✓ Are there many magazines in the office?
✓ Do you like fashion?
✓ Did you watch the movie?
Write 2-3 Questions
✅ Good Work!
Question patterns:
• Is there + singular noun?
• Are there + plural noun?
• Do/Does + subject + verb?
• Did + subject + verb?
Activity 4 — Your Final Challenge
🎯 Goal: Tell a short story about The Devil Wears Prada using your new English. Use the model as a starting point, then record yourself.
Model Story:
"I watched The Devil Wears Prada at the cinema. I liked the movie because it was interesting. There is a famous magazine called Runway. There are many beautiful clothes in the office. Miranda is very strict. Andy is nervous but intelligent. Do you like fashion movies?"
Your Story (Write First, Then Record)

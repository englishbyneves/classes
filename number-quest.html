Use your numbers for real

Four missions. Speak out loud each time — the frame helps, the reveal checks.

Mission 1 · your age

How old are you?

Set your age, then say the full sentence out loud.

How old are you? — I'm ___ years old.
24
Mission 2 · your number

What's your phone number?

In English we say each digit, one by one. Type yours, then read it out loud.

What's your number? — It's ___.

Tip: for 0 say "oh" or "zero".

Mission 3 · at the café

Order and ask the price

Tap a price to see how to say it. Then order out loud.

    How much is the ___? — It's ___ reais.
    I'll have ___ ___, please.
    You: How much is the coffee?
    Staff: It's eight reais.
    You: I'll have two coffees, please.
    Staff: That's sixteen reais.
    Mission 4 · what time?

    Talk about your day

    Tap a time to see how to say it. Then talk about your routine.

    What time do you wake up? — At ___.
    A: What time do you start work?  B: At nine. I finish at six.

8Home Practice

Do these during the week. Tap each one when it's done.

Record yourself. Record a 30-second audio introducing yourself. Use at least one help phrase, like "Can you repeat, please?" or "How do you say… in English?"
Mirror talk. Say your introduction out loud 3 times in front of the mirror.
Name things. Pick 5 objects at home and ask out loud: "How do you say ___ in English?"
Sticky note. Write one help phrase on a note and keep it where you study.

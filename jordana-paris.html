Stage 4 · The Pattern 8 min

Happening right now

One little pattern lets you describe anything you're doing this second:

I'm + _____ing
"I'm" = I am · the action gets -ing on the end
I'm walking 🚶‍♀️ I'm drinking coffee ☕ I'm taking a photo 📸
Careful
I walking.
Say this
I'm walking.

💡 Don't lose the little "I'm" — it's the engine of the sentence.

Stage 8 · Pronunciation 4 min

The -ing sound

It's soft and at the back: "-ng", not "-ng-uh". Say each one slowly, then fast.

walk·ing drink·ing tak·ing smil·ing danc·ing
🎵
Trick: say it like a little song — keep your mouth relaxed, no hard "g" at the end.
Stage 9 · Checkpoint 3 min
